The file antenna.gra provides important physical features of receiving
antennas:

- the position of the antenna reference point (ARP) to which the phase center
  correction values in the corresponding IGS antenna phase center model
  (currently igs08.atx available at ftp://igs.org/pub/station/general/) refer.
  The ARP is preferably an easily accessible point on the lowest non-removable
  horizontal surface of the antenna. Typically, the ARP coincides with the
  axis of attachment of the antenna to a monument or surveying instrument.

- the definition of the north reference point (NRP), i.e. the element of the
  antenna that has to be oriented toward the true north direction.

- antenna dimensions (in meters) or other measurable features.

At the end of the file, a machine-readable quick reference section is given
for convenience. Antenna names are defined in the file rcvr_ant.tab (also
available at ftp://igs.org/pub/station/general/). The IGS Central Bureau, with
support from the IGS Antenna Working Group, maintains this list and should be
contacted at support @ igs.org with any comments.


ARP abbreviations
=================
BAM: bottom of antenna mount
BCR: bottom of chokering
BDG: bottom of dome ground plane
BGP: bottom of ground plane
BPA: bottom of preamplifier
TCR: top of chokering
TDG: top of dome ground plane
TGP: top of ground plane
TOP: top of pole
TPA: top of preamplifier


NRP abbreviations
=================

Major features
--------------
MMI: man-machine interface
NOM: north orientation mark (placed on antenna by manufacturer)
RXC: receiver connector (connect antenna to external receiver)
UNK: unknown

Secondary features
------------------
BAT: battery compartment door release
BTD: bottom of tear drop shape (wide end)
CMP: mounted compass
DIS: display/digital readout (DIS is a specific type of MMI)
DRY: cap or cover for drying agent
PCS: PC card slot
TMT: tape measure tab or notch for slant height pole

Connectors
----------
CAC: nonspecific cable connector (only allowed for legacy calibrations)
CTC: external controller connector
DAC: data cable connector (for data collectors besides external receivers)
PWC: power port; external power connector
RTC: RTK connector; UHF connector for RTK broadcasting antenna
RXC: receiver connector (connect antenna to external receiver)


Dimensions
==========
All dimensions are in meters.
